How can we help you?

Subscribers exclusively enjoy unlimited access to all of The Atlantic's journalism. You can choose from three subscription plans, all of which are available for purchase online:

Digital - Includes unlimited site access, The Atlantic's iOS and Android app, a subscriber-only newsletter with weekly insights from our newsroom, exclusive live events, professionally narrated audio articles, and a digital library with PDF issues of the magazine.
Upon purchasing a subscription, you can expect it will take a few weeks for your first print issue of the magazine to arrive. Issue delivery will depend on your location.

If you're a subscriber located in the United States
  • You can expect to receive your first issue 4-6 weeks from order date
If your're a subscriber located in Canada
  • You can expect to receive your first issue 6-7 weeks from order date
If you're a subscriber located outside of the United States or Canada
  • You can expect to receive your first issue 7-8 weeks from order date
If you'd like to read an issue of The Atlantic, you can access dounloadable pdf editions of the magazine through your Digital Library. Here's how to access your digital library, on the website or in the app.

Note: Delivery to certain areas could be temporarily interrupted due to currrent Covid-19 pandemic
You can read The Atlantic on virtually any device. We've included instructions for a few common devices here.

Web Browser
Anyone can read The Atlantic's website from any device that is connected to the internet and has access to a web browser. Simply open your web browser and navigate to www.theatlantic.com.

Digital subscribers to The Atlantic can also access their Digital Libraries from the web browser on any device that is connected to the internet. More information about accessing your Digital Library can be found here.

iOS Devicees
To read The Atlantic on iOS or Apple devices:
  1. Open theatlantic.com in your preferred web browser. Atlantic subscribers can read unlimited articles or download digital magazine issues to Apple devices.
  2. Or you can use The Atlantic's iOS app, available to download in the App Store. All subscribers have unlimited access to the app when they sign in with their Atlantic account details. If you'd like to use this option, please find further instructions here. Please note: The Atlantic app requires iOS 13 or newer.
Android Devices
To read The Atlantic on Android devices:
  1. Open theatlantic.com in your preferred web browser. Atlantic subscribers can read unlimited articles or download digital magazine issues to Android devices.
  2. Or you can use The Atlantic's Android app, available to download for free in the Google Play Store. All subscribers have unlimited access to the app when they sign in with their Atlantic account details. If you're not yet a subscriber, please purchase a subscription on theatlantic.com. Please note: The Atlantic app requires Android 6 or newer.
Kindle By Amazon
To read The Atlantic on a Kindle e-reader:
  1. Open theatlantic.com on Kindles with web browser capabilities.
  2. Digital subscribers can download digital magazine issues to Kindles with web browser capabilities.
  3. Purchase a specific Kindle subscription to The Atlantic through Amazon.
Your subscription through Amazon includes all print editions of The Atlantic magazine in Kindle format, but not unlimited access to The Atlantic's website. To enjoy full digital benefits, please explore our subscription options.

Nook By Barnes And Noble
To read The Atlantic on Nook by Barnes and Noble:
  1. Open theatlantic.com on Nooks with web browser capabilities.
  2. Digital subscribers can download digital magazine issues to Nooks with web browser capabilities.
  3. Purchase a specific Nook subscription to The Atlantic through Barnes and Noble.
Apple News+
Unfortunately, you cannot currently access The Atlantic using your Apple News+ subscription. You also cannot access Apple News+ using your Atlantic subscription.
